The E Ching

August ~ Septemeber, 2015

Just for fun

Enjoy a free digital simulation of the preeminent form of ancient divination.

A CSS sprite animation gives life to coins you can flip with the press of a button. Choose from a selection of coins, follow the prompts, and recieve some relevant sage-like advice.

JavaScript produces 3 * 6 random coin flips, which builds a random I Ching hexagram, and displays a reading of that hexagram.

Galactic Odometer

2014 ~ 2015

Just for fun

This JavaScript powered odometer calculates and outputs a running count of the space miles you've traveled as the Earth and galaxy orbit.

Just enter your date of birth, and watch the vast miles fly by.

Complete with cool animations and a space-styled explanation of the math and speeds involved in coming up with milage approximation.

My Skills

I am a perpetual student of Web Development. Between the middle of 2014 and the end of 2016, I have accumulated approximately 2,000 hrs developing or learning to develop for the web.

At present I have a firm grasp on mobile-first, responsive design, cross-browser compatibility, and the basic skills needed to build a functioning website. And I'm always learning more.

With HTML 5, I try to write markup that is clean and semantic.

With CSS and Illustrator, I aim to create styles that can match any mood or theme.

With JavaScript, I manipulate the DOM, create programs, and add functionality and user interaction in a number of creative ways.

With GitHub, I manage development and version control, as well as multi-device testing.

Currently, I am studying development tools and more of the tools in the JavaScript family (Angular, Node, AJAX, jQuery, Express, NPM, MongoDB).

My plan is to further my education by learning server-side technologies (the MEAN stack), CMS, frameworks, preprocessors, and task runners (Wordpress, LESS, Grunt), and 3D in the browser, (HTML Canvas, three.js, WebGL).
