ME

BIO

Geetings! My name is Robert Manolis, but you can call me Bobby. I am a thirty-eight year old married father of three and a Front-End Web Developer and Designer.

I am a writer, artist, philosopher and humanist.

My education didn't stop at the classroom. I've been studying the arts and sciences with a passion for most of my life. In the summer of 2014, I dove into Web Development and Design, and I'm having the time of my life.

I view each new project as an opportunity to create a work of art. I approach each new project with passion and an open-mind. And then I commit myself to the timely completion of a product that meets or beats expectations.
